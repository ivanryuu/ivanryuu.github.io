Rubik's Cube Solver

The cube solver shows 4 different views of a cube in order to simulate a 3-D cube. The panel includes four buttons that allow the user to create a shuffled cube, a previous and next in order to cycle through the move list, and finally a solve button which will automate the solving of the cube by traversing the solve move list shown at the bottom of the app.
